Unit Page

Test before production

This unit focuses on how digital models reduce risk, improve planning, and support better manufacturing decisions.

Essential Questions

  • How can digital models reduce manufacturing risk?
  • What is a digital twin?
  • How can simulation improve motion, layout, safety, and efficiency?
  • Why test virtually before building physically?

Learning Targets

  • I can explain the purpose of a digital twin.
  • I can use digital evidence to identify potential problems.
  • I can connect CAD/CAM decisions to physical outcomes.
  • I can document simulation results and revisions.

Unit Deliverables

  • CAD model, layout, or simulation evidence
  • Motion study or process screenshots
  • Digital twin explanation
  • Risk/problem analysis
  • Revision plan and reflection
