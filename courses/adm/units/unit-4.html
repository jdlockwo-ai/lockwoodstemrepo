ADM Unit 4

Securing the Supply Chain

Students explore cybersecurity, data integrity, secure software, demand planning, procurement, production scheduling, packaging, accessibility, and resilient manufacturing.

View Lessons Unit Challenge Back to ADM
Unit Goal

Protect the system

This unit focuses on how manufacturers protect products, data, people, timelines, quality, and delivery systems from disruption.

Essential Questions

  • Why is cybersecurity important in connected manufacturing?
  • How can data, software, and human decisions disrupt production?
  • How do demand, procurement, scheduling, and packaging affect supply chain performance?
  • How can manufacturers design systems that prevent, respond to, and recover from disruptions?

Learning Targets

  • I can explain the CIA triad in a manufacturing context.
  • I can identify data, software, cybersecurity, and human-factor risks.
  • I can analyze how demand, procurement, scheduling, and packaging decisions affect production.
  • I can recommend improvements that increase manufacturing resilience.

Major Deliverables

  • Engineering notebook entries
  • CIA triad or cybersecurity analysis
  • Demand/procurement/schedule evidence
  • Packaging or accessibility review
  • Risk analysis and recommendations
  • Resilient Manufacturing reflection

Unit 4 lesson clusters

The unit moves from cybersecurity and data integrity to supply chain planning, packaging, accessibility, and a resilience challenge.

4.1

Cybersecurity, Data Integrity & Secure Software

Students explore how cybersecurity affects connected machines, sensors, software, data, people, and production environments.

Activity 4.1.1CIA Triad

Learn how confidentiality, integrity, and availability apply to connected manufacturing systems and production data.

Activity 4.1.2Human in the Lab

Activity 4.1.3Malware

Open Lesson
Activity 4.1.4Problems With Data

Analyze how missing, incorrect, corrupted, or poorly formatted data can affect manufacturing decisions and product quality.

Activity 4.1.5Secure Software

Explore how software design, updates, access control, and secure coding habits help protect automated systems.

Project 4.1.6Malfunctioning Lab

Activity 4.2.2Procurement
