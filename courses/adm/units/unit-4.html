ADM Unit 4

Securing the Supply Chain

Students study cybersecurity, supplier decisions, production planning, packaging, logistics, quality control, risk, and manufacturing resilience.

View Unit Structure Back to ADM
Unit Page

Protect the system

This unit focuses on how manufacturers protect products, data, timelines, and quality across the entire production and delivery process.

Essential Questions

  • How do supply chains affect manufacturing decisions?
  • Why is cybersecurity important in connected manufacturing?
  • How do companies reduce production and delivery risk?
  • How do quality control and packaging protect products?

Learning Targets

  • I can map a manufacturing supply chain.
  • I can explain how cybersecurity risks affect production.
  • I can compare suppliers using cost, quality, lead time, and risk.
  • I can justify a quality-control or packaging decision.

Unit Deliverables

  • Supply chain map
  • Supplier comparison or decision matrix
  • Risk analysis
  • Quality-control plan
  • Packaging/logistics plan
  • Final recommendation
